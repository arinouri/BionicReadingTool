Read Easier

with

A collection of tools designed to improve your reading focus, speed, and accessibility.

Bionic Reading

Boldens parts of words to guide your eyes and boost reading speed.

Spritz Reader

Displays one word at a time for reduced eye movement.

Chunking

Breaks large blocks of text into manageable chunks.

Text-to-Speech

Convert written text into spoken words.

Line Highlighting

Highlights one line at a time to reduce distractions.

Reader View

Removes clutter and distractions from web pages.

Colored Overlays

Apply visual filters to make reading easier for dyslexia and more.
